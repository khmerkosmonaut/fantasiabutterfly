FAQ

How do you source your butterflies?

We get our butterflies from overseas. Families farm them, none are taken from their environment.

How are the butteflies euthanized?

They are frozen, which causes them to close. We rehydrate them, spread them and then they are air-dried.

How long do they last?

They can last indefinitely, but they are parishable. For the longest life keep away from humidity, drafts and direct sunlight.
